# Employee Handbook

## Leave Policy

Employees receive 15 days of annual leave after probation.
Sick leave requests must be submitted in WorkOS before 10:00 a.m.

## Remote Work

Regular employees may work remotely up to three days per week.
New hires in their first 90 days must work on-site at least four days per week.

## Security Training

All employees must finish security training within 14 days of onboarding.
