# Benefits Overview

## Leave and Flexibility

Full-time employees receive 12 days of annual leave in the first year.
The hybrid work arrangement allows up to two remote days per week.

## Onboarding Support

New hires receive a mentor for the first 30 days.
新進同仁需在到職後七天內完成資訊安全課程。

## Claims Support

福利手冊只說明公司提供醫療與心理健康資源，沒有列出正式理賠審核天數。
